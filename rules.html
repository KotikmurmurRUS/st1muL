VLN FEST 2023: Basic rules

1. COMMUNICATION & RESPECT

1.1 Be respectfull when talking with staff or someone else. 1.2 No aggresion expected when communicating. 1.3 Fight, aggresivity is strongly forbidden. 1.4 Use default English & Dutch when talking to our staff. 1.5 Do not disturb our staff when they are busy. 1.6 Vandalism, same with (laptops, tablets,...) not allowed. 1.7 Food & non-alcoholic drinks allowed, alcohol forbidden. 1.8 Pick the correct way to enter the festival zone. 1.9 Do not touch or hit the stage, keep distance >1.5m. 1.10 You are not being expected near or behind the stage. 1.11 Drones & other fly apparatures forbidden for safety.
