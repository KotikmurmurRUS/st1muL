DOCUMENTATION

We are a new (virtual) musical festival organisation, to let people celebrate special days with a high-quality stage & favourite DJ's! This place is a perfect option for those who is searching for a vibe or want celebrate with new friend there. We present to our readers virtual events though a LIVE stream, letting people listen to the talents of DJ's. Physical events is recommended for teens (16-), you might pay the price at the entrance. This website is used to watch our upcoming events & news live. We also have social media accounts, the content from our events will be posted on social media. Our festival terrain has some basic & additional rules, don't break them. By asking or suggesting something, contact us on our social media servers (Whatsapp/Discord). If you're searching for a job, security/operator job available by filling up the register form on our collaborate page (see navigation bar). We always look for the best locations to not disturb residents and animals. We hope this organisation will grow the population of our city.
