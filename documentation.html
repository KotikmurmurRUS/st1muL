DOCUMENTATION

Valena Festival is a brand new musical festival, with small stages but big vibes. Inlcuding few events private & public in a year. This festival will be not organised as a full & real musical festival. Our stages doesn't take any terrain to run the events. This festival is and will be organised originally for people searching for a musical vibe & those who wants to celebrate the special day with some music talents. Recommende to be -16, follow our given rules available on our screen's & website. PUBLIC EVENTS contains standing/driving bicycle with a small DJ set on it, and short routes to drive as sporting. PRIVATE EVENTS contains private content that's available only for our choosen people. That type of event will be organised with more expensive & sized stage components, in- & outdoor.
